You're a Mean One

 AUDIO-ONLY VERSION

 John recorded this instrumental version with his Fender "Jazzmaster" electric guitar (multi-tracks) and programmed bass & drums (Roland synth). The arrangement is based on the original version of the 1966 cartoon: How the Grinch Stole Christmas. The music was written by Albert Hague.

The Fender Jazzmaster was created as a more up-market instrument than the Stratocaster and it was originally supposed to replace the Telecaster model. John's was built in 1960 and originally purchased by his grandfather as a studio guitar for his own recording studio in North Hollywood.
